Hello, welcome to a page about 3 pokemon.

Here we are going to go over 3 different types of pokemon. Starting with and introduction to each of them, along with a photo and a litte bit about them.

Magikarp

Magikarp is a silly little red fish that doesnt do much besides flop around and eat burgers.
Its main attacks are splash and tackle, there are rumors that after it eats enough burgers it will evolve into a monsterous sea serpent.

Psyduck

Psyduck is a duck with a horrible constant headache, it is confused for most of its life and suffers greatly from it.
They say the only thing to help calm the headache and aid the suffering of these poor ducks is lots of burgers.

Jolteon

Jolteon is made of half dog and half electricity.
Making it perfect for shocking people with its teeth until it gets a burger.
